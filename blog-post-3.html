⟡ BEHIND THE SCENES: PAYNAIJA PROJECT ⟡

A case study by MOTIVLAB

Published: August 15, 2025 | By Tunde Adeyemi

Image Placeholder

Introduction

The PayNaija project was a transformative branding initiative for a rising fintech company in Nigeria. At MOTIVLAB, we took on the challenge of creating a visual identity that reflects trust, innovation, and accessibility. This case study dives into the process and outcomes.

Project Overview

PayNaija aimed to simplify digital payments for Nigerian users. Our goal was to design a logo, color scheme, and marketing materials that would resonate with a diverse audience while establishing a strong market presence.

Design Process

We began with extensive research into fintech trends and user preferences. The process included:

  • Sketching multiple logo concepts.
  • Developing a vibrant palette with amber and teal tones.
  • Creating mockups for mobile and web applications.
  • Gathering client feedback through iterative reviews.

Results

The final branding was launched in mid-2025, resulting in a 30% increase in user sign-ups within the first month. The logo and materials received positive feedback for their modern and approachable design.

Conclusion
